# Project Thesis

## Positioning

`TrialMatch Audit` demonstrates applied AI engineering for biotech by turning trial eligibility into an auditable decision pipeline rather than a generic chatbot.

## Product claim (honest + strong)

The system performs **protocol-fit auditing**, not real-world enrollment prediction. It answers:
- "Given this patient profile and trial criteria, where does the patient fit or fail?"
- "What evidence supports this decision?"

## Why this is recruiter-relevant

- shows schema-first systems design,
- uses deterministic baselines before LLM reasoning,
- creates traceable outputs with explicit error classes,
- supports rapid iteration from prototype to production-like architecture.

## Non-goals (for now)

- no claim of clinical safety certification,
- no claim of true site-level screening outcomes,
- no hidden black-box decisions without rationale.
